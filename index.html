About Gura

Profile & Background

Gura is a descendant of the Lost City of Atlantis, who swam to Earth while saying "It's so boring there LOLOLOL!" He bought her outfit (and shark-shaped beanie) in the human world and she really likes it. In his free time, he enjoys talking about marine life.

Gura was introduced simultaneously on hololive's English Twitter account and hololive's Japanese Twitter account as well as also on hololive's English YouTube channel in a small video with the rest of the first generation on September 8, 2020. Twitter account- Hers was made in July, but she didn't make her first tweet until her official introduction on September 8. Her YouTube channel was created on July 16, but she debuted on September 13.

Gura's popularity quickly rose, becoming the fastest growing VTuber ever. On October 20, just 37 days after his debut, Gura overtook Shirakami Fubuki and Inugami Korone to become the number 1 most subscribed hololive VTuber on YouTube. On October 22, Gura became the first hololive member to reach 1,000,000 subscribers, the third VTuber overall after Kizuna AI and Kaguya Luna , and the first English-speaking VTuber to do so.

Personality

Gura is friendly and easy to like, and often entertains his audience with silly antics. He has no sense of direction, frequently misspells and pronounces words incorrectly, has difficulty remembering his age, and consistently fails to solve basic arithmetic equations, leading viewers to affectionately call him a "stupid shark".

He is also chronically lazy at times, as in one instance he left a raw egg in the sink under a plate for 3 weeks before noticing a foul smell and fuzz around it, and admitted to eating baby food "because you don't have to cook it, you don't have to chew it." You know! Just eat!" as well as being caught naked during his pre-debut video meeting with fellow Gen-mates, which made him the butt of jokes.

However, despite his terrible flaws, Gura is intelligent, and has extensive knowledge of Shark Facts. When asked why he wasn't "boing boing", he justified it by claiming that he was "hydrodynamic". His skill with fast-paced rhythm games is extraordinary, and he is very ambitious. He occasionally makes raunchy jokes, and is familiar with modern meme culture.
